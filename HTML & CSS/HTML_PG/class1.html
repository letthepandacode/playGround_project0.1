Thambi Vilas was a small establishment from a South Indian Village, now has found its way to establishing an outlet in the heart of Chennai.

It explores long lost South Indian Cuisine with finest ingredients and raw materials.

The menu here is more realistic for everyone to visit this outlet as a habit to indulge and enjoy the food with unconditional love.
